How MARN Works
Your complete guide to finding the perfect rental or listing your property

For Tenants
Find your perfect home in four simple steps:
1. Search & Filter: Browse thousands of verified listings with advanced filters for location, price, amenities, and roommate preferences.
2. Schedule Tours: Book virtual or in-person property tours at your convenience. View detailed photos and 3D walkthroughs.
3. Connect & Chat: Message property owners and potential roommates directly. Get answers to all your questions instantly.
4. Book & Move In: Complete your application online, sign the lease digitally, and pay securely. Move into your new home!

For Property Owners
List and manage your properties with ease:
1. List Your Property: Create a detailed listing with photos, descriptions, and amenities. Set your price and availability.
2. Screen Tenants: Review verified tenant profiles, background checks, and references. Choose the best match for your property.
3. Communicate: Chat with interested tenants, schedule tours, and answer questions through our secure messaging platform.
4. Manage & Earn: Accept payments, handle maintenance requests, and track your earnings all from one dashboard.

Why Choose MARN?
Features that make your rental experience seamless:
Verified Listings: All properties are verified for authenticity and accuracy.
Secure Payments: End-to-end encrypted payment processing for your safety.
Compatibility Matching: Smart algorithm matches you with compatible roommates.
Market Insights: Access real-time market data and pricing analytics.
