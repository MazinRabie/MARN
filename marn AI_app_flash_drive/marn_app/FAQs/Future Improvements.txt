## Future Improvements

**What features are planned for future versions of MARN?**

- AI-Assisted Verification: Automated review of identity and ownership documents to speed up the verification process and detect inconsistencies
- AI-Based Price Estimation: A pricing model to help owners set fair rental prices and help renters evaluate offers based on market data
- Geographical Expansion: Extending the platform beyond Egypt to support other countries, currencies, and languages
- Handwritten Digital Signature Support: Allowing users to sign contracts with a handwritten-style signature for a more natural experience
- Voice-Enabled Assistant: Adding voice interaction to the chatbot for improved accessibility
- AI-Assisted Reporting & Violation Detection: Using AI to help detect abusive messages, misleading listings, and repeated policy violations
- Property Sales Support: Expanding MARN beyond rentals to support full property purchases
