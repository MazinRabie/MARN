## Identity Verification

**Why do I need to verify my identity?**
Verification ensures platform trust and legal compliance. Unverified users cannot create property listings or enter into rental contracts.

**What documents are required for verification?**
- Renters: A valid legal identification document (e.g., National ID)
- Owners: National ID plus proof of property ownership documents

**How does the verification process work?**
1. Upload your documents from the Profile Settings or verification section
2. The system validates the file format and stores documents securely
3. Documents are submitted for administrative review
4. You will be notified once your verification is approved or rejected

**How long does verification take?**
Verification is reviewed by MARN administrators. You will receive a notification as soon as a decision is made.

**What happens if my verification is rejected?**
You will be notified of the rejection. You can re-upload corrected documents and resubmit for review.
